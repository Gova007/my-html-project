About the City

Kodaikanal is known as the "Princess of Hills" and is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, India. It's renowned for its natural beauty, including lakes, waterfalls, and forests. The town is situated in the Palani Hills, part of the Western Ghats, at an elevation of 7,200 feet (2,100 meters).
